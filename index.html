▸ How to play
The Countdown Numbers Game
You are given 6 numbers and a 3-digit target. Using any combination of those numbers with +, −, ×, ÷, try to reach the target exactly — or get as close as possible.

Rules:
  • Each number may only be used once.
  • You don't have to use all 6.
  • Every intermediate result must be a positive whole number.
  • Division must produce a whole number.

To play: click two tiles to select them, then click an operation (+, −, ×, ÷). The result replaces those two numbers. Keep going until you hit the target!
